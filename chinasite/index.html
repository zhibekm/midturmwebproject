    ABOUT US

    Launched and conceptualized in 2015, EAST WEST EDUCATION was set out to assist students with a desire to travel and study abroad. Quality is important to us and we pride ourselves on the excellent support we offer. Having personal experience in all our programs lets us understand the students’ needs.

    Read More

    Our vision

    We believe that travelling changes your life. It broadens your horizons, teaches you to live in the present moment, changes your perspective and transforms you. There is no greater feeling than having your bags packed and knowing you’re ready to go on your next adventure. Travel has the power to be truly meaningful and finding that meaning is why we do what we do.

    Our values

    Since our establishment we have successfully assisted to more than 1000 students to experience the life abroad. Many of our participants come to us through word of mouth. Thanks to all our clients we are growing and offering new programs every year.

    Our purpose

    Living abroad gives the opportunity to experience a place on a deeper level. It challenges your perception, encourages cultural understanding and creates lasting friendships. Never stop improving yourself – that is what we believe in and apply in our daily life.
